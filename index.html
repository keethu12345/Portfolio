About me

With a B.Tech in Computer Science (Big Data Analytics) from SRM IST, Chennai, and a BS in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras, I have a deep passion for machine learning, intent-based networking, NLP, and data analytics.

Currently, I'm engaged in two exciting projects at IIT Madras: one involves automated text generation on Kubernetes data using GPT and BERT models, while the other focuses on identifying and classifying PFAS sources using machine learning. I also enjoy working on personal projects, like my election data analysis using Python tools.

My goal is to contribute to research in ML and computer networks. When I'm not immersed in tech, I love teaching and fashion designing, which fuel my creativity and keep me grounded. I'm driven by curiosity and a desire to push the boundaries of what's possible.
